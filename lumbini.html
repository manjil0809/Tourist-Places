Lumbini - The Birthplace of Lord Buddha

Discover the Sacred Lumbini

Lumbini is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the birthplace of Siddhartha Gautama, who later became known as Lord Buddha. It attracts millions of pilgrims and tourists from around the world, seeking peace and enlightenment. It is home to temples, gardens, and monuments dedicated to Buddha's birth.

Maya Devi Temple

A revered temple marking the exact spot where Buddha was born.

Lumbini Garden

A peaceful garden that offers a tranquil atmosphere for meditation and relaxation.

Ashoka Pillar

An ancient pillar marking the spot where Emperor Ashoka visited and declared Lumbini a sacred site.
