Chitwan National Park - Wildlife Safari Adventure

Explore the Wildlife of Chitwan

Chitwan National Park is one of the best places for wildlife safaris in Nepal. The park is famous for its population of one-horned rhinoceros, tigers, and elephants. The park offers various activities like jeep safaris, bird watching, and canoeing.

Elephant Safari

Enjoy an exciting ride through the jungle, spotting wildlife up close on the back of an elephant.

Canoeing in Rapti River

Take a boat ride along the Rapti River and spot various species of birds and animals.

Tiger Tracking

Track tigers in their natural habitat with a professional guide on a thrilling adventure.
