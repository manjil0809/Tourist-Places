Explore Pokhara - A Tourist's Paradise

Discover Pokhara's Beautiful Tourist Spots

Pokhara, a beautiful city in Nepal, is famous for its stunning natural beauty, serene lakes, and panoramic views of the Himalayas. The city offers a range of sightseeing opportunities, from peaceful lakes to adventurous treks and rich cultural experiences.

Phewa Lake

One of the most beautiful lakes in Nepal, offering boating experiences and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Sarangkot

Famous for its panoramic views of the Annapurna range, best visited for sunrise or sunset.

Devi's Fall

A stunning waterfall located in Pokhara, with a mysterious legend associated with it.

Gupteshwor Cave

A sacred cave, home to a shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva, located near Devi's Fall.

World Peace Pagoda

A massive Buddhist stupa offering a panoramic view of the city and Phewa Lake.

Tal Barahi Temple

Located on an island in Phewa Lake, this temple is dedicated to the Hindu goddess Barahi.
