Patan - The Ancient City of Art and Culture

Discover the Rich Heritage of Patan

Patan, also known as Lalitpur, is one of the three ancient cities of the Kathmandu Valley. It is renowned for its rich history, beautiful temples, and artistic legacy. The Patan Durbar Square is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its medieval Newar architecture and intricate carvings. Patan is also famous for its metalwork, particularly for making statues and religious artifacts.

Patan Durbar Square

The heart of Patan, showcasing stunning palaces, temples, and courtyards from the Malla period.

Hiranya Varna Mahavihar

Also known as the Golden Temple, it is a beautiful Buddhist monastery with remarkable architecture and religious significance.

Mahabouddha Temple

A temple dedicated to Lord Buddha, known for its intricate terracotta sculptures and historical importance.
